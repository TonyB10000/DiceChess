Dice Chess Rules

Goal: Capture the opponent's King Cube.

Mobility & Power: Die face value = Mobility (max squares it can move). Lower value = more Power (can only capture pieces with an equal or higher face value).

Action Points (AP): You have 6 AP per turn (White gets 3 AP on turn 1).

  • Move (3 AP): Move a piece up to its face value.
  • Capture (5 AP): Move and capture an eligible enemy piece.
  • Increment (1 AP): +1 to face value (more mobility, less power). Max 6.
  • Decrement (1 AP): -1 to face value (less mobility, more power). Min 1.

The King Cube: Blank face. Neutral power (can capture/be captured by anything). Moves 1 square for 3 AP (can move twice per turn, e.g., in an L-shape).

Advancement Score (Anti-Stalling): To prevent stalling, your Advancement Score (pieces closer to the enemy side score more points) MUST be strictly higher at the end of your turn than at the start.

Suspension of Advancement: The Advancement Score rule is suspended if you perform a Capture. This allows for tactical retreats and lateral strikes.

How to Use the App

  • Moving: Tap a piece to select it, then tap a highlighted square to move or capture.
  • Inc/Dec: Use the ▲ and ▼ buttons to modify a selected piece's value.
  • Auto-End Turn: If your turn is legally valid and you reach 0 AP, the turn ends automatically!
  • Reset Turn: A rounded blue button appears once you spend AP. Use it to undo mistakes and restart your current turn.
  • Invalid Alarm: If your AP reaches 0 (or you make an illegal trivial King move) and your turn does not satisfy the Advancement Score, a flashing red alarm will appear. You must press Reset Turn!
