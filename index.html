Dice Chess Rules

The Objective: Capture opponent's King, force Checkmate, or force a "Freeze Out" (cannot satisfy Advancement Score).

1. Power vs. Mobility

  • Pieces are 6-sided dice (1-6) and a King (♔).
  • Mobility: Move up to face value (orthogonally/diagonally). Kings move 1.
  • Power: Attacker value must be ≤ target value. ('1' captures anything; '6' captures '6'/King). Kings have infinite power.

2. Action Points (AP)

6 AP per turn (White starts with 3 AP). Split among any pieces.

  • Modify (1 AP): +1 or -1 face value (1-6).
  • Move (3 AP): Move to empty square.
  • Capture (5 AP): Move onto enemy square.

3. The Advancement Score (AS)

Prevents stalling by forcing board progression.

  • AS is the sum of how far pieces have moved.
  • Net AS at turn end must be strictly higher than turn start.
  • Exception: Captures suspend AS rule for that turn.
  • If actions don't satisfy AS or capture, turn is illegal.
